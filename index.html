Blood Bowl Sevens - Rules

Kick Off

The game starts with a kickoff. The ball is placed in the middle of the pitch and scatters 1-4 squares in a random direction.

Turns

Each half has 8 turns per team (16 turns total per half). The game has 2 halves (32 turns total). Click 'End Turn' when you have completed all player actions for your turn.

Players have 2 minutes to take their turn.

Player Actions

Each player can do 1 action each turn:

  • Move: Move up to your movement allowance. You can move through tackle zones but must dodge to leave them.
  • Block: Block an adjacent opponent. Block dice are modified by assists.
  • Blitz: Move and then block an opponent (costs 1 square of movement). Can only be used once per turn.
  • Pass: Move and then pass the ball to a teammate. Can only be used once per turn.

If an action fails (e.g., failed pass, failed catch, failed 'Go For It', player knocked down), there is a turnover and the turn ends immediately.

Go For It

During movement, you can attempt to move 2 additional squares beyond your movement allowance. For each extra square, roll a D6. On a 1, the player falls over and it's a turnover.

Tackle Zones

When moving, squares adjacent to opposing players are in tackle zones. Moving into or through tackle zones requires a dodge roll. Leaving a tackle zone gives a -1 modifier to the dodge roll.

Block Modifiers

Block dice are modified by assists. Players can assist if they are adjacent to the target and not engaged with other opponents. Each assist adds +1 to block dice.

Pass Modifiers

Pass distance affects the catch roll: 0-3 squares (no modifier), 4-6 squares (-1), 7-9 squares (-2), 10+ squares (-3). Maximum pass distance is 13 squares.

Player Placement

Before kickoff, teams place their players in their half of the pitch.

Objective

Score a touchdown by moving a player with the ball into the opposition endzone.
